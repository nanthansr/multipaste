# Multipaste

**Multipaste is the rapid-fire paste buffer for your next 10 seconds - not your next 10 days.**

While other clipboard managers focus on long-term retrieval, Multipaste sits invisibly in the background, designed specifically for rapid sequential copying and pasting.

![Multipaste Demo](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nanthansr/multipaste/main/demo.gif)

## Features

- **Cycle & Drop:** Hold `Cmd+Shift`, tap `V` to cycle through recent clips. A tooltip appears exactly at your text cursor. Release to instantly paste.
- **FIFO Sequential Paste (Pro):** Enable FIFO mode, copy items A, B, C in order, then tap `Cmd+V` three times to paste them in exactly that order.
- **Privacy First:** Never captures 1Password/Bitwarden passwords. Ignores concealed and transient clipboard types.
- **Multi-format (Pro):** Copy and cycle through images and files, not just text.

## System Requirements
- macOS 13 Ventura or newer

## Installation

1. Download `Multipaste-1.0.0.dmg`.
2. Drag `Multipaste.app` into your `Applications` folder.
3. Launch `Multipaste`.
4. Grant Accessibility permissions when prompted (required to intercept global hotkeys).
